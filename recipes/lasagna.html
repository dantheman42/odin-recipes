Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian pasta dish made by layering flat sheets of pasta with meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese, then baked until bubbly. Traditionally made with a ragù alla Bolognese sauce, there are also vegetarian and seafood versions.

It's a popular dish worldwide, enjoyed in various forms across different countries. Its versatility and satisfying nature make it a favorite for family gatherings, casual meals, and special occasions. Bellissimo!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Pork
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Herbs
  • Béchamel sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna sheets until al dente; drain and set aside.
  3. Sauté onions and garlic until soft; add ground meat and cook through.
  4. Pour in tomatoes, herbs, and seasoning; simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Melt butter in a pan; whisk in flour until smooth.
  6. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until thickened.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  8. Layer pasta, meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese; repeat.
  9. Finish with a top layer of cheese.
  10. Bake for 30-40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
